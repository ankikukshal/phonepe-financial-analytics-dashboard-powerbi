# 📱 PhonePe Financial Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

An interactive **PhonePe Financial Analytics Dashboard** built in **Power BI** to analyze digital payments, insurance, loans, money transfers, recharge & bill services, transaction status, and failed payment reasons.

This dashboard converts fintech transaction data into **business intelligence insights** for tracking customer usage, service adoption, revenue contribution, and payment success trends.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights

* 💰 **Total Transaction Amount**
* ✅ **Successful Transactions**
* ❌ **Failed Transactions**
* 📈 **Monthly Transaction Trends**
* 💳 **Payment Success vs Failure**
* ⚠️ **Failed Payment Reason Analysis**

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights

* Recharge & bill payments dominate total transaction volume
* Money transfer remains one of the highest-value services
* Insurance and loan products show stable monthly growth
* Failed payments are mainly due to PIN/server-related issues
* Seasonal spikes highlight peak fintech usage periods
